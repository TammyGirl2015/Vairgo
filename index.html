Africa

From safari's in Kenya to the beautiful coral reef's in Zanzibar to the Cape Town waves, Africa is my favorite place to travel! The local people are friendly and it is a relatively safe place to travel solo. If you want to get up close and personal with nature, this is a must do!


Europe

Shopping, modern art, picturesque homes. Europe is ideal for travel hopping. Inexpensive flights from one country to another within Europe: have breakfast in Paris, lunch on a Schnizel in Germany by noon and be in Switzerland in time for chocolate and dessert (and some nightlife if you fancy!)


Asia

If you're an Instagram follower, you know i love food. Aisa is hands down the best place to go to fully experience cuisine from street food to fine dining. Asia also offers ancient history and culture that is evident throughout your time there. My experience in India was very "eat, pray, love."


North America

Before i go further, while i do love hidden gems, i can appreciate why some popular destinations are popular: they're simply awesome! While i have been to Las Vegas, Maine offers some wonderful lakes and scenic views, while Oregon has some amazing rock climbing spots for the adventurous.
